Guacamole

A twist on the tradition!

You don't have to be of Latin heritage to enjoy this recipe!

Make authentic guacamole just like someone's abuela.

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados-peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 roma tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons fresh chopped cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic.
  3. Stir in cayenne pepper. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor.
