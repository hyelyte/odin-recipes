Mashed Garlic Cauliflower

Love cauliflower again!

Are you someone that hates plain old cauliflower? Let's change your mind!

This recipe will have you coming back for more.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic, or to taste
  • 2 pounds frozen riced cauliflower, thawed
  • 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch nutmeg, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon snipped fresh chives for garnish

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet, over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  2. Stir in thawed, riced cauliflower, salt, pepper, and nutmeg. Add chicken broth and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
  3. Cook, covered, about 8 minutes, then remove cover and continue cooking and stirring until most of the liquid evaporates, an additional 5 to 8 minutes.
  4. Use an immersion blender to purée cauliflower until smooth. If an immersion blender is not available, spoon cauliflower into a blender to purée. When smooth, place in a serving bowl and garnish with snipped chives.
